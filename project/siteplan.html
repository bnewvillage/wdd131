Site Name

Site Name: Blaine Villanueva

This name is chosen because for all intents and purposes this website is all about Blaine Villanueva as a individual.

Site Purpose

A professional online presence that helps showcase skills, projects, and contact information to potential collaborators or employers. Improve my chances of networking and career opportunities.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: How can I get in touch with Blaine for a project collaboration?

Scenario 2: What professional skills does Blaine have, and what projects has he worked on?

Color Schema

  • Darkest Teal (#040D12): Used for the primary background.
  • Dark Teal (#183D3D): Used for footer and secondary backgrounds.
  • Muted Green (#5C8374): Used for buttons and highlights.
  • Very Light Teal (#E6F2ED): Used for headings and body text.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat for modern look.
  • Body Font: Arial for readability.
  • Accent Font: Roboto (used for call-to-action buttons and labels).
